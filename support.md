Need help with Tiny Triumphs? We're happy to help.

## Contact us

Email **[support@bean-studio.com](mailto:support@bean-studio.com)**. Please
include your account email and a short description of what's happening, and a
screenshot if it's a visual issue.

## Common questions

### How do I invite another caregiver?
Open a baby's care team and create an invitation. The other caregiver enters the
invite code in their app. Invitations expire 15 minutes after they're created and
can be used once.

### How do I export my data?
You can share or print your logs from within the app.

### How do I delete my account and data?
You can delete your account from within the app, which removes your baby profiles,
logged events, schedules, and account record. If you can't access the app, email
**[support@bean-studio.com](mailto:support@bean-studio.com)** from your account
email address and we'll process the deletion for you.

### How do I manage or cancel a subscription?
Subscriptions are billed by Apple or Google. Manage or cancel them in your App
Store or Google Play account settings.
