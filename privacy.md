This policy explains what personal data the Tiny Triumphs app ("the app")
collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. The app is provided by Bean
Studio Apps ("we", "us"), the data controller, located at 60 Paya Lebar Road,
#06-28, Paya Lebar Square, Singapore 409051.

## Summary

- We collect your account email and the baby activity you choose to log.
- Data is stored on your device and synced to a cloud database. Access rules are
  enforced at the database level so that a baby's log is available to its owner and
  the caregivers invited to it.
- The app includes no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
- We do not sell your data or use it to train machine-learning or AI models.
- You can export your activity logs (CSV or PDF), and delete your account and its
  data, at any time.

## Who this policy is for

The app is used by parents and caregivers to record information about their
children. The account holder is an adult. The data you log may describe an infant
or child (for example, sleep and feeding times), but it is created and controlled
by you, the account holder. See [Children's data](#childrens-data) below.

## What we collect

**Account information.** When you sign up we store your email address and an
authentication record so you can log in and so caregivers can be invited by email.
You can sign in using Apple or Google; in that case, Apple or Google handles the
sign-in and provides us your email address. We do not require your name, phone
number, or postal address.

**Data you create in the app.** The information you choose to enter, which may
include:

- Baby profiles you create (such as name and birth date)
- Sleep, feeding, and other activity events you record
- Schedules and routines you set up
- Caregiver invitations and team membership (the email addresses of people you
  invite to share a baby's log)

**Purchase records.** If you buy the one-time Pro unlock, payment is processed by
Apple's App Store or Google Play — we do not receive or store your payment-card
details. We store a record of whether your account has unlocked Pro, validated
through the store.

**Technical data.** Our hosting provider processes standard connection information
(such as IP address) to deliver the service and protect against abuse.

## Where your data is stored

The app keeps a copy of your data **on your device** (in local storage) and syncs
it to a **cloud database provided by Supabase**, our database, authentication, and
sync provider. Supabase processes this data on our behalf, on cloud infrastructure
located in the Asia-Pacific (Tokyo) region.

Access is governed by **row-level security** — rules enforced by the database so
that a given baby's log is accessible to its owner and the caregivers explicitly
invited to that baby. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

## International data transfers

If you use the app from the EEA or the UK, your data is transferred to Japan to be
hosted there. Japan is recognised by the European Commission and by the UK as
providing an adequate level of data protection, which provides the legal basis for
this transfer.

## Third parties that process data for us

We use the following service providers ("sub-processors"). They process data only
to provide their service to us, not for their own purposes:

- **Supabase** — database, authentication, and real-time sync hosting.
- **Apple** and **Google** — sign-in (if you choose Apple or Google sign-in) and
  processing of the Pro purchase through their app stores.

The app does **not** include third-party advertising, analytics, or attribution
SDKs.

## How we use your data

We use your data to:

- Provide the app's features (logging, schedules, reports, and caregiver sync)
- Authenticate you and keep your account secure
- Validate your Pro purchase
- Respond to your support requests

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or to train
machine-learning or AI models.

## Legal basis for processing

Where the GDPR (or UK GDPR) applies, our legal bases are: **performance of our
contract** with you, to provide the account and features you sign up for and to
validate your Pro purchase; and our **legitimate interests** in keeping the
service secure and preventing abuse, for limited technical and connection data.
Where we ever rely on your **consent**, you can withdraw it at any time.

## Caregiver sharing

The app lets you invite other caregivers to share a baby's log. The caregivers
sharing a baby can see the activity logged for that baby and the email addresses
of the other caregivers on that team. Invitations expire 15 minutes after they are
created and can be used once. You can remove a caregiver at any time, which revokes
their access.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account is active. You can delete your account and its
associated data — baby profiles, logged events, schedules, and account record —
from within the app, or by emailing us (see
[Support]({{ '/support/' | relative_url }})). After deletion, residual copies in
routine encrypted backups are purged within 30 days as those backups age out of our
provider's normal rotation.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the EU/UK GDPR), you may have the
right to:

- **access** the personal data we hold about you;
- **correct** data that is inaccurate;
- **delete** your data;
- **export** a copy of your data (data portability);
- **object to** or **restrict** certain processing.

Much of this is available directly in the app — you can export your activity logs
and delete your account at any time. For anything else, email us and we will
respond. You also have the right to **lodge a complaint with your local data
protection authority** (in the EEA or UK, your national supervisory authority).

## <a id="childrens-data"></a>Children's data

The app is intended for use by adults to record information about their own
children. Accounts may only be created by adults. We do not knowingly collect
personal information directly from children. The app is not directed to children
under 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction) and is not part of Apple's
Kids Category. Information about a child that appears in the app is entered and
controlled by the parent or caregiver who owns the account, and can be deleted by
them at any time. If you believe a child has created an account with us, please
contact us and we will remove it.

## Security

We protect data with encryption in transit, database-level access rules
(row-level security), and authentication on every request. No method of storage or
transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If a
breach affects your personal data, we will notify you and the relevant authorities
as required by applicable law.

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top and,
where appropriate, notify you in the app.
